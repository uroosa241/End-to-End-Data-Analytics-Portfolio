


# End to End Data Analytics project (SQL + Python)

## Overview

I developed this project as a complete end-to-end data analytics practice and solved real-world business problems using SQL and Python.
This project combines both **SQL-based business analytics** and **Python-based data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA)** to create a strong analytical workflow.

---

## What I Worked On

In the first phase of the project, I used **Python** to clean and prepare the dataset. This included handling missing values, removing duplicates, and performing basic data validation. I also conducted **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** to understand the structure of the data and identify key patterns and anomalies. Additionally, I applied **outlier detection techniques** to ensure data quality before analysis.

After preparing the data, I moved on to **SQL analysis**, where I solved a wide range of business questions ranging from basic reporting to advanced analytical problems.

---

## SQL Analysis Covered

The SQL section of this project includes structured problem-solving across multiple levels:

At a basic level, I analyzed fundamental business metrics such as total customers, orders, revenue, product distribution, employee counts, and payment status.

At an intermediate level, I focused on business performance insights including top customers, best-selling products, revenue by category, employee performance, return rates, and sales distribution across time and geography.

At an advanced level, I worked on real-world analytical problems such as identifying high-value customers, repeat buyers, customers with no orders, products never sold, and return behavior analysis.

At a more advanced (FAANG-style) level, I implemented complex analytics including customer lifetime value, monthly revenue trends, running totals, churn detection, demand trends, and conversion analysis.

Finally, at the senior analyst level, I built advanced business logic such as customer segmentation (VIP, Regular, Lost), RFM analysis, cohort analysis, retention rate calculation, revenue leakage due to returns, sales funnel analysis, and executive KPI dashboard queries.

---

## Key Business Insights

Through this project, I was able to derive meaningful business insights such as:

* Understanding customer purchasing behavior and segmentation patterns
* Identifying revenue growth trends over time
* Measuring customer retention and churn behavior
* Evaluating product performance and return patterns
* Analyzing employee contribution to revenue
* Tracking overall business KPIs for decision-making
